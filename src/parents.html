E.A.K for Parents

Help prepare your child for the new digital era

E.A.K. is a revolutionary way to inspire kids to code. This brand new Mario-style game introduces real coding languages, encouraging both girls and boys aged 8+ to become creators, rather than consumers of technology. You can even spend quality time playing and learning to code alongside your child, since E.A.K. is for beginners (and technophobes!) of any age.

£4/child/year

Benefits:

  • Helps to eliminate the fear that most girls have of coding, technology and engineering*
  • Teaches both computational thinking and real coding languaghes e.g. HTML, CSS and javascript (used in all websites and webapps)
  • Helps to build up essential life skills that support your childs intellectual and creative development
  • Allows you to play and learn coding skills alongside your child, and share levels that you've built together.
  • New content released regularly (every 8-10 weeks)
  • Kitten gifs! (there are lots more in the full version)
