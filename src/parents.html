Give your child an advantage

E.A.K. provides a brand new way for kids to learn how to code. This fun, Mario-style game introduces professional coding languages, inspiring both girls and boys aged 8+ to become creators, rather than consumers of technology.

You can even spend quality time playing and learning to code alongside your child, since E.A.K. is for beginners (and technophobes!) of any age.

  • Teaches computational thinking and professional coding languages e.g. HTML
  • Allows you to play and learn coding skills alongside your child, and share levels that you’ve built together
  • Helps to eliminate ‘technophobia’
  • Builds up essential life skills that support your child’s intellectual and creative development
  • New content released regularly (every 8-10 weeks)
  • Kitten gifs! (there are lots more in the full version)
