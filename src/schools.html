E.A.K. for Schools

Help prepare your students for the new digital era

E.A.K. is pioneering as it teaches programming in a real-world, creative context. This learning tool helps students build up personal learning thinking skills which are crucial for lifelong success in learning. E.A.K. is designed to inspire and encourage students to become researchers, teachers, problem solvers, team builders, writers and designers, as well as coders.

E.A.K. will also allow teachers to become facilitators of independent learning, as the game is incredibly engaging to students aged 8-14

£1/Student/year

Benefits to students:

  • Knowledge of both computational thinking and real coding languages e.g. HTML, CSS and Javascript.
  • Additional skills complementing and going beyond the National Curriculum (e.g. PLTS)
  • New content released regularly (every 8-10 weeks)
Coming soon: Knowledge of all concepts in the UK KS2 and KS3 computing curriculum and concrete application of these concepts

Benefits to teachers:

  • An extremely effective method to engage girls and boys aged 8-14 in computing
  • Allows teachers to become facilitators of autonomous, independent learning
  • New content released regularly (every 8-10 weeks)
Coming soon: An easier, more efficient method of teaching and assessing the KS2 and KS3 computing curriculum via lesson plans and built in tools for teachers
