Help prepare your students for the new digital era

Unlike other coding tools, E.A.K. combines teaching professional coding skills, with personal, learning and thinking skills that are essential for success in learning.

This highly engaging game helps students to become autonomous, independent learners, and makes it easy for teachers to become facilitators of independent learning.

E.A.K. will offer students:

  • Knowledge of all of the concepts in the UK KS2 and KS3 computing curriculum and concrete application of these concepts
  • Additional skills complementing and going beyond the national computing curriculum - students become researchers, teachers, problem solvers, team builders, writers and designers, as well as coders
  • Knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript - real coding languages used by professional developers
  • The ability to create real websites and apps
  • A high level of engagement around computing
  • New content released regularly

E.A.K. will offer teachers:

  • An easier, more efficient, method of teaching the KS2 and KS3 computing curriculum due to E.A.K. lesson plans and an extremely high level of engagement from all students.
  • An easier, more efficient method of assessing the KS2 and KS3 computing curriculum, due to build-in tools for teachers to easily measure the progress of their students
  • A far hgiher level of engagement in computing from girls in Years 4, 5 and 6.
  • A method to engage students in independent learning.
